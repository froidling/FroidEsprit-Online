Links

I tend to find many interesting people and resources online. Here are a few.

Ham Radio

  • Delta Amateur Radio Society - My primary club affiliation, a great gang of elmers.
  • Radio Amateurs of Canada - My national amateur radio advocacy organization, and my secondary club affiliation.
  • Ham Radio Crash Course (YouTube) - One can't really talk about ham radio online without mentioning Josh KI6NAZ. I got my callsign because of his community.
  • DX Engineering (YouTube) - Likewise, DX Engineering is the premier starting point for amateur hardware.
  • QRP Labs - Hans G0UPL does great work designing powerful and affordable gear.

VR and Tech

  • Dilmer Valecillos (YouTube) - Virtual Reality developer advocate I'm a fan of. He spoke at a developer seminar I attended in 2024.

Software I Like

    Radio

  • fldigi - Digital amateur radio software to allow for control of equipment via sound card.
  • HAMRS Pro - A simple yet comprehensive logging app, useful for POTA activations while camping.
  • Robot36 - SSTV decoder for phones. Another POTA tool, I love it for satellite transmission.
  • Morse Mania by Dong Digital - A simple morse code training app, popular among my club members.

    Music Listening & Creation

  • MusicBee - My desktop music player software and MP3 device manager of choice
  • Musicolet - Likewise, my mobile music player of choice.
  • SyncThing - I use this app to syncronize the music folders on my PC and phone when both are on WiFi.
  • Synthesizer V Studio 2 Pro - My vocal synth software of choice. I use Megpoid GUMI and Liam voices, or I would if I could get a DAW working without fuss. Gumi just sits on my desk these days, staring at me until I'm productive.
